## Variant: Editorial-minimal

### Design stance
The numbers ARE the design. Massive typography and extreme whitespace communicate confidence — the protocol's health is self-evident.

### Key choices
- **Layout:** Single-column, full-width with generous padding. No cards or boxes — data sits directly on the void background.
- **Typography:** 52px light-weight metric values, 13px uppercase labels. Extreme contrast ratio creates hierarchy without borders.
- **Color:** Accent gold reserved for the single most important number (TVL). Everything else in grayscale.
- **Interaction:** Click a vault row to expand an inline detail panel below it. Click again to collapse. Create Vault triggers a blurred overlay.

### Trade-offs
- **Strong at:** Instant emotional read of protocol health. Feels premium and confident.
- **Weak at:** Data density — can't show many vaults at once without scrolling. Not for power users managing 50+ vaults.

### Best for
- Landing / marketing-facing dashboard. First impressions.
- Users with 1-10 vaults who want a clean overview.
- Protocol itself as a "state of the union" display.

ZENO · Tides of Patience

Bitcoin is a sea. Time is the only ship that crosses it.

You are a Keeper. Lock treasure and Bitcoin into a Vault and sail 1129 days across the Sea of Volatility. Endure the crossing and draw 1% every 30 days, forever — a withdrawal that shrinks but never reaches zero. Zeno's paradox, made seaworthy.

Five rival Keepers sail beside you. When they lose their nerve and abandon ship, their forfeited Bitcoin rains on every Keeper still afloat. Patience is the meta.

Mint — lock treasure + a chosen amount of BTC
Abandon — bail early, forfeit the rest, lose the treasure
Strip — once vested, carve collateral into vBTC, 1:1
Draw — after vesting, take 1% per 30 days
Match rain — quitters' losses become your gains
Raid — claim a rival's abandoned reserve, 1:1

A seeded price simulation — no real Bitcoin, no wallet, no risk. A sandbox for testing the Zeno Greenfield protocol.
